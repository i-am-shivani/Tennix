What Is TENNIX

As the world becomes increasingly digitized, it's easy to overlook the importance of face-to-face social connections. This is why qe have created a social network driven by the common love of racquet sports.

We build healtheir and more active communities where people connect, interact and earn rewards from playing the sports they love, together.

Our Vision

At TENNIX, we believe that everyone should be rewarded for living an active,healthy lifestyle.We want to bring people together who share passions and interests, using racquet sports as the driving force.

Connect

Wherever you are! Find other members of the TENNIX community around the world. Expand your social circle, network professionally and enjoy yourself at the same time.

Play

Game on! Match the perfect partner and play either socially or competitively. Schedule game at your mutual convenience and build relationships in the process.

Earn

Cash in! Explore exciting opportunities to earn TENNIX tokens. Our 'Play-To-Earn' and 'Coach-To-Earn' features allow you to earn by playing matches, taking part in tournaments or coaching others to improve their game.

Shop

Treat yourself! Spend your hard earned tokens on a range of products and experiences in the TENNIX Marketplace.
